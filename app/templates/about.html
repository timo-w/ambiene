About Ambiene

Ambiene is my final-year University project and part of my dissertation in Computing Science & Music at the University of Glasgow.

At its core, Ambiene is an online music collaboration and creation project. Users can come and make relaxing ambient music together by controlling the many different controls of the app. It's built with Django Channels using WebSockets and is deployed on fly.io.

See more on the GitHub page!
